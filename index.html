Course Reflection

01

Introduction

Taking this course was something I did not expect to enjoy at first, mostly because I thought it would just be another English class. But as the semester went on, I realized that it actually helped me in ways that go beyond just reading and writing. It made me see that if i want to be good at my field i also need to enhance my communication skill as well as my english skills, which is something I never really thought about before.

02

One thing I got out of this course was learning how to read technical texts without getting lost. Before, whenever I would come across long or complicated reading materials, I would easily lose track of what they were saying. Learning how these kinds of texts are usually structured helped me follow along better and actually understand what I was reading.

03

Another thing that improved was the way I think when I read. I started asking more questions about the content instead of just reading to finish. That change made a big difference because I was no longer just going through the words and not actually know it. I was actually processing and thinking about what the word meant, which made discussions and activities a lot easier for me.

04

Writing summaries and analyses also helped me a lot more than I expected. It forced me to really understand what I read before I could write about it, and that process made the lessons stick better. I also put ideas into my own words without relying too much on copying how the original text was written which i don't usually do before.

05

Conclusion

Overall, this course left a good impression on me. It was one of the difficult courses i take, but it's very engaging and i have learned a lot taking this course. I feel like the habits I picked up here reading more carefully, thinking critically, writing clearly and putting my ideas into the text are things I will carry with me for a long time.
